Admissions

How to Apply

Applying to Little Crest is simple. Visit our school office for an admissions pack or download the forms below. Our team will guide you through every step.

  • Collect or download the application form
  • Complete the form and attach required documents
  • Submit to the school office for review
  • Await admission decision and joining instructions

Admission Requirements

To ensure a smooth enrollment, please have the following ready:

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Latest school report form or transcript
  • 2 passport-sized photos
  • Completed admission form

Fees & Payments

Our fee structure is competitive and designed to offer value in line with our commitment to quality education. Detailed fee schedules are available at the office or on request.

  • Flexible payment plans available
  • Bank deposit slips required for all payments
  • Receipts issued upon payment confirmation
