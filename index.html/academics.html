Mercy of Outreach Community-Based Organization (C.B.O) High School

The High School program at Mercy of Outreach Community-Based Organization (C.B.O) prepares students for national examinations (KCSE) and beyond. We emphasize academic rigor, mentorship, and personal responsibility, ensuring students excel both in studies and character.

  • Qualified subject-specialist teachers
  • Science labs, library, and digital resources
  • Career guidance and university prep
  • Leadership development through clubs & councils
