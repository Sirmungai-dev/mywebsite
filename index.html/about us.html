About Mercy of Outreach Society

ABOUT US

Mercy of Outreach C.B.O is a faith-driven community organization based in Dandu, dedicated to transforming lives through education, compassion, and service. We provide integrated Islamic and formal education to orphans, vulnerable children, and underprivileged families who cannot afford quality schooling. Founded on the principles of mercy, unity, and service to humanity, the organization envisions establishing one of the leading centers for Islamic and modern (secular) education ̶ nurturing morally upright, intellectually empowered, and socially responsible future leaders.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in integrated Islamic and modern education ̶ empowering the less fortunate to acquire knowledge, strengthen their faith, and build a brighter future.

Purpose and Objectives

Supporting the Vulnerable: To provide material, financial, and emotional support to people with disabilities, widows, orphans, and economically disadvantaged individuals within the community. Community Empowerment: To build a strong, supportive community where members work together to address challenges and improve the quality of life for everyone

Our Core Values

• Compassion: Guided by mercy, love, and care for the vulnerable. .Integrity: Committed to honesty, accountability, and transparency in all actions. • Faith: Grounded in Islamic teachings, values, and moral discipline. • Excellence: Pursuing quality and professionalism in education and service delivery. • Community Empowerment: Building strong, educated, and resilient communities. • Inclusivity: Ensuring equal opportunities for all, regardless of background or circumstance.
